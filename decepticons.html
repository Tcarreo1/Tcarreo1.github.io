Who are the Decepticons?

In the Transformers universe, the Decepticons are a faction of sentient alien robots defined by their pursuit of power, domination, and control. Originating from the planet Cybertron they are typically portrayed as the antagonists, opposing the more peace-driven Autobots. While the Autobots fight to preserve freedom and coexistence, the Decepticons believe strength and authority are the natual order of the universe. Across countless timelines animated series, comic books and block buster films, the Decepticons are constantly reimaged, with new members joining and others fading away. Despite this ever changing roster, a core group of Decepticons consistently appears in nearly every iteration, forming the backbone of the faction's identity.

Their main enemies are the Decepticons, a rival group of Transformers led most often by Megatron. While the Decepticons seek power and domination, the Autobots fight to stop them and preserve peace across the galaxy.

Across the long history of the Transformers universe, there are hundreds of members of the Autobots. Different TV shows, comics, films, and toy lines introdue new characters regularly, expanding the ranks of Autobots far beyond the small core team that most audiences first encounter. Despite the constantly growing roster, a small group of Autobots appears consistently across many different versions of the franchise. These characters often serve as the backbone of the Autobot team regardless of timeline or adaptation.

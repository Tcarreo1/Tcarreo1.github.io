For my Webpage Project, I have gone with a wiki based concept that provides information on the well known science fiction series called the Transformers. The Main page consists of a summary of the Transformers lore mostly revolving around the Michael Bay live action iteration of the series. It dwelves into the beginning of the Transformers history. Telling of how the seemingly endless war between the two Transformers factions, Autobots and Decepticons, started.

The Autobots page of the project tells of the Autobots side of the story. What they are fighting for, their beliefs and ideals. What they did before the war started and what they do now. How Transformers who were once Cybertronian librarians or consruction workers are now soldiers in the war against the "Evil" Decepticons. Led by their fearless leader and the last Prime, Optimus Prime.

The Decepticon page of the project tells of the Decepticon side of the story. What they are fighting for, their beliefs and ideals. Their stories before the war and what they went through before the war started. They too were once regular Cybertronian civilians living day to day life before having to become soldiers in the never ending war between the autobots. Now led by their ruthless leader, Megatron, they fight mostly out of fear but some out of loyalty.
